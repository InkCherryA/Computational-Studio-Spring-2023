The Red Strings Club
Deconstructeam (Game)
The first scene is extremely epic. It begins with a man falling from a building sighing for his death. He ends with saying “are you listening.” Then flashback starts which is the story itself. He begins by saying “are you listening.” The same words are put into totally different scenarios, one epic, one casual. It hints at the romantic relationship between main characters while startling the viewer emotionally.
This game does very well in posing philosophical, which in many cases ethical, problems. The language used is fairly well polished. The conversations always go smoothly despite allowing choices for the player.
